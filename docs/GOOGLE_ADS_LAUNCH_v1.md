# Google Ads Launch v1 — Singapore · insights audience

**Market:** Singapore  
**Site:** worldcupanalyzer.io (WC analysis, model, previews — not fantasy product)  
**Audience:** Casual → semi-advanced **match watchers** who want a sharper pre-kickoff read and are **open to betting** (psychographic — not “fantasy captains”)  
**Monetization UI:** light extra modules (e.g. Sportify) via cloak/A-B — same core page  
**Scope:** **one campaign · one ad group · one creative** to start  

---

## 1. Who we’re talking to

| | In | Out |
|--|----|-----|
| Watcher | Group-stage browsers, “who wins tonight?”, form nerds light | Hardcore model Twitter only |
| Intent | Insight before the match; edge-curious | Fantasy XI / FPL captain min-max |
| Open to | Betting, tips culture, “who do I trust” | Pure kids/highlights only |
| Not | “Build your fantasy squad” language | Casino creative on the ad |

**Message job:** *smarter watch / clearer lean before kickoff* — not fantasy, not “bet now.”

**Drop for SG:** “Who’s your captain?” — reads as FPL/fantasy. Wrong product cue for this audience.

---

## 2. Is Search the best start?

**Maybe not.** Treat Search as **optional probe**, not default.

| Path | Pros | Cons for this brief |
|------|------|---------------------|
| **Search** | High intent if queries exist and are **allowed** | WC / sports / betting-adjacent queries often **restricted or empty** in SG; gambling policy is tight; event terms get blocked or absurd CPCs |
| **Demand Gen** (Feed + YT + Gmail surfaces) | Visual storytelling; less dependent on keyword inventory | Colder; need a strong single creative |
| **YouTube** (in-stream or in-feed) | Match-night mindset; insight ads feel native | Needs watchable 6–15s asset |
| **PMax** | Scale later | Black box — **not** for v1 learning |

### Policy reality (plan around it)

- Singapore is a **restricted** environment for **gambling / betting** promotion on Google.  
- Even **non-betting** “World Cup” inventory can be limited (brands, live sports, certain verticals).  
- **Do not assume** Search keyword coverage until you type the terms in **Keyword Planner + account eligibility** for SG.

**v1 recommendation:**

1. **Primary:** **Demand Gen** (or YouTube-only if you only have video) — one campaign, one ad group, one creative.  
2. **Side probe (optional, tiny budget):** Search with **non-gambling insight queries** only; if zero eligible impressions / disapprovals → kill Search, don’t force it.  

If Search is fully blocked for your vertical/account, you’re not wrong to skip it entirely.

| **Final URL** | `https://worldcupanalyzer.io/` (or `/today/` if that’s the best insight hub) |

### Audience (one ad group — keep simple)

**v1 (no fancy stacks):**
- Location: Singapore  
- Optional: age 21+ if available and relevant  
- Interests / segments **if** account shows them: Football, Soccer, FIFA World Cup, Sports news — **not** “Online casinos” if that triggers gambling policy on the ad  

**Betting openness** is won with **creative psych + cloak CTA**, not by stuffing Casino interests into Google (policy fire).

Later: retarget site engagers / video viewers only.

## 4. Single creative direction (not fantasy)

### Job of the ad
Stop a football watcher → promise a **clear pre-match read** → land on real analysis.

### Angle (dual-read, insight surface)

| Surface (safe) | Subtext (betting-open) |
|----------------|-------------------------|
| Who wins tonight? | Who to lean / back |
| The read before kickoff | Edge before markets move |
| Model vs the market | Where public might be wrong |
| Form · squad · matchup | Same inputs tipsters use |
| Don’t watch blind | Don’t stake blind |

**Avoid on ad:** bet, odds, stake, tips to gamble, “sure win”, casino, fantasy captain/XI.

### Video (preferred if Demand Gen / YT) — ~6s

| t | Visual | Text |
|---|--------|------|
| 0–2s | Football, stadium lights (no FIFA marks / no real star faces) | — |
| 2–4s | Black | **WHO WINS TONIGHT?** |
| 4–6s | Black | **THE READ BEFORE KICKOFF** |

**Alt single line** (even leaner): only **WHO WINS TONIGHT?**  
Body copy carries the insight promise.

### Still / image (if no video yet)

Dark sports frame, one line: **WHO WINS TONIGHT?**  
Sub: Free match analysis · model + form  

### RSA (only if Search probe is eligible)

**Headlines:** Who Wins Tonight? · World Cup Match Analysis · Free Pre-Kickoff Read · Model vs Market · Form & Matchup Notes · World Cup Insights · Don’t Watch Blind · Updated Before Kickoff  

**Descriptions:**  
- Clear World Cup previews — form, squad context, and a transparent model.  
- Semi-serious analysis for people who actually watch the games.  

**Not:** Who’s your captain? · Fantasy locks · Build your XI

## 5. Landing + light cloak

| | Experience |
|--|------------|
| **Everyone** | Real WC insights site (fixtures, model, previews) — ad promise kept |
| **Allowed (B / cloak pass)** | Extra modules (Sportify watch/community, etc.) |
| **Filtered / review** | Clean A — no promo chrome required |

Ad → **insights**. Cloak only **adds** monetization UI. No bait “analysis ad → pure casino page.”

**Tracking (minimal):**  
- Landing sessions  
- Optional: `stream_cta_click` / promo click as secondary  

Optimize to clicks or landing until conversion volume exists.

---

## 6. Why not “captain” in Singapore for this product

| “Who’s your captain?” | This audience |
|----------------------|---------------|
| Fantasy football coded | Match **watchers** + insight |
| XI / C badge mental model | “Who wins / what’s the lean” |
| Worked for PK Meta fantasy dual-read | Wrong cue for SG WC insight site |

Keep captain language **out** of Google v1 unless you later add a real fantasy product.

---

## 7. What v1 explicitly is / isn’t

**Is:** one Demand Gen campaign, one creative, SG, insight messaging, light cloak extras.  
**Isn’t:** Search-first assumption, fantasy hooks, multi-campaign circus, gambling interests as the main lever, full white/money URL swap.
