The 2026 World Cup is the biggest in the tournament's history: **48 teams**, **104 matches**,
and three host nations across North America. Whether you watch every kick or just want to
know who's going to lift the trophy, this is the place to follow along.

## What makes 2026 different

For the first time, the group stage is built around twelve groups of four. The top two from
each group advance, along with the eight best third-placed teams — 32 sides into a
single-elimination knockout that ends in New Jersey on 19 July.
